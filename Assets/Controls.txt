--- Overworld ---

WASD/Arrow Keys: Move
Shift/Control (press/toggle on/off): Sprint
E (currently only w/ picnic basket object): Interact

--- Battle ---

Point and click w/ mouse: Select [button/action/target]
Escape, Backspace, or Delete: Back

--- Editor Tools ---

Shift + Z: Toggle game speed up
Ctrl + Shift + Z: Reset game speed to default
